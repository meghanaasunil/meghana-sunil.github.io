Medical AI & Neuroinformatics

  • * Graph-Guided Cross-Modal Representation Learning of EEG–fNIRS via Spatio-Temporal Transformers for Stress Analysis

    Meghana Sunil · Submitted to Computer Methods and Programs in Biomedicine · Advisor: Dr. Jeetashree Aparajeeta

    CM-GGT: a cross-modal gated fusion framework with spectrogram-transformer EEG encoders and graph-based fNIRS encoders, achieving 93.2% accuracy and 0.967 AUC on simultaneous EEG–fNIRS stress recordings and outperforming 11 state-of-the-art baselines.

  • * Encouraging Discriminative Attention Through Contrastive Explainability Learning for Lung Cancer Diagnosis

    Meghana Sunil · IEEE Access · Advisor: Dr. Nachiyappan S

    Contrastive Explainability Learning (CEL) framework for lung cancer classification, enforcing Grad-CAM– based contrastive constraints to encourage discriminative attention while maintaining >98% accuracy and F1-score on the IQ-OTH/NCCD dataset.
